About Holophrame

What is a Holophrase?

A holophrase is a single word that expresses a complete thought. Think of how a child might say "up!" to mean "pick me up" or "water!" to mean "I want water." It's the essence of communication stripped to its core.

Holophrame embraces this philosophy: meaningful communication without the noise.

Our Philosophy

No Algorithms

Your feed shows posts in chronological order. Period. We don't decide what you should see based on "engagement" or advertiser interests. You see what's happening now, in the order it happened.

Minimal Data Collection

We collect only what's necessary: username, email, and password. That's it. No phone numbers. No real names required. No tracking pixels. No selling your data.

Brutalist Design

Holophrame looks like the web from 1995, and that's intentional. No smooth animations. No hidden navigation. No algorithmic trickery. What you see is exactly what you get.

Text-First Communication

Posts are limited to 500 characters. This forces clarity and thoughtfulness. We believe in the power of words, not just images and videos competing for attention.

Technical Details

Holophrame is built with minimal, transparent technology:

  • Frontend: Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript - no frameworks
  • Backend: Node.js with Express and MongoDB
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with 7-day expiry
  • Real-time: WebSocket for live updates
  • Open Source: Code available for audit (coming soon)

The Rules

  1. Be human. Real people, real thoughts, real conversations.
  2. Character limit: 500. Say what matters, nothing more.
  3. No spam. Automated posting will get you banned.
  4. No harassment. Disagree all you want, but be civil.
  5. No manipulation. No fake accounts, no vote brigading, no gaming the system.

Who Built This?

Holophrame was built by developers who remember when the web was weird, wonderful, and human-scale. We're tired of algorithmic feeds, dark patterns, and surveillance capitalism masquerading as "connection."

This is our attempt to build something different. Something honest.
